The Maison

Founded 1998, Milan

A House Built on Silence

AURELIA was born from a single conviction: that true luxury speaks in a whisper. Founded in 1998 in a quiet atelier on the Via Montenapoleone, our house has spent nearly three decades rejecting the obvious in favour of the enduring.


Every garment that bears our name has passed through the hands of master craftspeople who treat their work as devotion. We source nothing that is not exceptional, and we compromise nothing that matters.

"Elegance is not about being noticed — it is about being remembered."
The Nocturne Collection

Drawn from Vienna's Dusk

Our 2026 collection, Nocturne, finds its soul in the fin-de-siècle architecture of Vienna — in the ornate facades that cloak raw emotion, in the gilded interiors that speak of civilisations that refused to forget beauty.


Each silhouette is an exercise in restraint. Double-faced cashmere overcoats. Hand-rolled silk blouses. Goodyear-welted shoes built to outlast a decade of trends. These are not garments. They are declarations.

"We do not follow the season. We set the conditions for it."
Our Philosophy
